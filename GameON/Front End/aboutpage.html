About

Game ON is a project for building a virtual betting scenario with a competitive set up to play against your friends.
This game does not involve any real money/currency, It is purely developed to play with virtual cash and enjoy the real time betting experience with live betting odds.

Rules


1. This Game can be played only on the occassions of any Internation Cricket Matches or Notable League Matches.

2. The Challenge Game for any cricket match starts 2 hours before the commencement of the match time

3. The Challenge Game can be played only against your friends.
